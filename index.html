About Us

Tradition Meets Taste

At Udupi Sri Krishna Naivedya, we believe great food brings comfort and happiness. Located in the heart of Yelahanka, our restaurant serves authentic Udupi‑style vegetarian dishes prepared fresh every day using quality ingredients.

From crispy dosas to soft idlis and wholesome meals, our focus is simple — clean food, honest pricing, and consistent taste.
